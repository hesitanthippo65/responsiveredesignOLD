Who Are We?

For over a century, Princeton in Asia (PiA) has been fostering mutual appreciation and cross-cultural understanding between the United States and Asia through immersive work fellowships in host organizations and communities. We work with host organizations in the fields of arts and culture, education, economic development, environmental sustainability, public health, sports, STEM, and peace, justice and access to information. We are invested in developing long-term partnerships with host organizations.
